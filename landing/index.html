Your Data is Sacred

Built secure from day one.
Not when we got big enough to care.

We know trusting an app with your business finances feels vulnerable. Here is exactly what we did about that.

🔐

End-to-End Encryption

Every request between your device and our servers is fully encrypted. Nobody can intercept your data.

🏠

Private Data Rooms

Your financial data sits in a private space only you can access. Row Level Security enforced at the database level.

🛡️

Two-Factor Authentication

Your account is protected with 2FA. Even if someone gets your password, they cannot get in.

✅

Payment Verification

All payment webhooks are verified with HMAC-SHA512 signatures — the same standard used by banks.

🚫

Zero Data Selling

We do not sell your data. We do not share it with advertisers. Your financial life is yours alone.

📱

Offline-First Storage

Your data is stored securely on your device too. Even without internet, your information stays protected.
