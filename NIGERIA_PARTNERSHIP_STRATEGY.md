# Nigeria Partnership Strategy for Quad360

## Executive Summary

Position Quad360 as the **financial clarity layer for Nigeria's fintech ecosystem**. Our goal: integrate with Nigeria's top payment processors and digital banking platforms to give SMEs the visibility they need to scale.

---

## Market Position

### The Nigeria Opportunity

**Market Size:**
- 41.3M SMEs in Nigeria
- 13.8M digital commerce users
- ₦15.6T annual transaction volume
- 15-25% annual growth rate

**The Problem:**
- 87% of SME owners don't know their real profit
- 64% can't access bank financing (can't prove profitability)
- 52% abandon cross-border sales (currency confusion)
- Average merchant churn: 18 months

**Quad360 Solves This:**
→ "See your profit. Scale your business. Get bank financing."
