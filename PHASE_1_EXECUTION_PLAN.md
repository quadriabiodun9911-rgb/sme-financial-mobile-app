## CONTINGENCY PLANS

### Risk 1: Zenith Bank Pilot Fails (Low Activation)
- **If:** Zenith pilot activation <30%
- **Then:** Root cause analysis immediately (UX issue? Education? Wrong user segment?)
- **Action:** Pivot to Access Bank (parallel track)
- **Timeline:** 1 week to determine, 2 weeks to course-correct

### Risk 2: Paid Acquisition CPA Too High
- **If:** CPA >₦100k (makes unit economics negative)
- **Then:** Pause paid spending, refocus on organic (referral + community)
- **Action:** Double down on viral loops, community events (cheaper)
- **Timeline:** Weeks 7–12, evaluate results by Week 12

### Risk 3: Platform Stability Issues
- **If:** Uptime <99%, error rate >2%
- **Then:** Halt all marketing, go into stability mode
- **Action:** Dedicate engineering team to fixing, delay feature work
- **Timeline:** Immediate response, target 48 hours to recovery

### Risk 4: Influencer Content Performs Poorly
- **If:** Influencer CTR <2% (below expected 5–8%)
- **Then:** Wrong audience selection or messaging
- **Action:** Switch to different influencer cohort (less lifestyle, more business-focused)
- **Timeline:** Week 7, execute new wave by Week 9

### Risk 5: Market Day Events Have Low Turnout
- **If:** <50 attendees per event
- **Then:** Venue selection problem or promotion insufficient
- **Action:** Partner with market association leader to co-host and promote
- **Timeline:** Week 5, all future events >100 attendees by Week 7
