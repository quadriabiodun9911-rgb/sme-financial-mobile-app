# QUAD360 × ZENITH BANK
## Executive Partnership Summary
**Prepared for:** The Office of the CEO / Executive Committee, Zenith Bank PLC

**Date:** June 2026 | **Classification:** Confidential

### THE PROBLEM

Nigeria's 41 million SMEs contribute 48% of GDP — yet over 90% have no formal financial records, and 74% of SME loan applications are rejected not for poor creditworthiness, but for missing documentation. Zenith Bank's SME portfolio carries elevated NPL risk and high origination costs precisely because these businesses are financially invisible.

### THE SOLUTION

Quad360 is a mobile-first Financial Operating System that automatically generates structured financial records for every SME that uses it — capturing transactions, computing real-time P&L, connecting bank accounts, advising through AI, and forecasting 12 months of cash flow. After 6 months of use, a Quad360 business has a complete, auditable financial profile equivalent to a management account — ready for credit assessment.

### THE OPPORTUNITY FOR ZENITH BANK

- **₦2.1 billion** in additional net interest income accessible from the pre-qualified SME pipeline in Year 3
- **35% reduction** in SME loan origination cost through pre-documented applicant files
- **20% reduction** in NPL formation rate through real-time portfolio monitoring and early warning signals

### THE FOUR PARTNERSHIP MODELS

| Model | What It Is | Timeline |
|---|---|---|
| **A — Co-Distribution** | Quad360 co-branded for Zenith SME customers as a free value-add | 60–90 days |
| **B — Loan Pipeline** | Pre-qualified, financially documented SME loan leads delivered to Zenith | 90–120 days |
| **C — Portfolio Monitoring** | Live financial health monitoring of existing Zenith SME borrowers | 90 days |
| **D — White Label** | "Zenith Business Manager" — fully branded proprietary SME financial tool | 120–180 days |

### FINANCIAL HEADLINE

Under the recommended combined Model A + B deployment, Zenith Bank is projected to generate **₦847 million in net new revenue** in Year 1, scaling to **₦4.3 billion by Year 3**, against a partnership investment of under ₦120 million over the same period. **ROI: 3,483%.**
