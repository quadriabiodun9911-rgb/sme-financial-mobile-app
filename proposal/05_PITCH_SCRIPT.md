# QUAD360 × ZENITH BANK
## 90-Second Executive Pitch Script
**For:** First meeting with Zenith Bank SME Banking / Executive team
**Speaker:** Quadri Abiodun, Co-Founder & CEO, Quad360
**Target time:** 88–92 seconds

---

## DELIVERY NOTES BEFORE YOU BEGIN

- Stand. Do not sit when delivering this opening.
- Speak at 60% of your normal speed. Slower than you think.
- Make eye contact with the most senior person in the room.
- No slides during the pitch. Slides come after.
- Pause deliberately at every [PAUSE] mark — these are not mistakes, they are power.

---

## THE SCRIPT

---

*[TAKE A BREATH. MAKE EYE CONTACT. BEGIN.]*

**"Every year, Zenith Bank turns away thousands of SME loan applications.**

**Not because those businesses cannot repay.**

**Because they cannot prove they can."** *(0:08)*

*[PAUSE — 2 seconds]*

**"Seventy-four percent of SME loan rejections in Nigeria cite one reason: missing financial records. Not bad credit. Not weak revenue. Just — no documentation."** *(0:18)*

*[PAUSE — 1 second]*

**"We built Quad360 to solve that — at the infrastructure level."** *(0:22)*

*[PAUSE — 1 second]*

**"Quad360 is a Financial Operating System for Nigerian SMEs. It automatically generates structured financial records — P&L, cash flow, invoice history, expense categorisation — as a byproduct of the business owner's daily activity. No accountant. No spreadsheet. Just a mobile phone."** *(0:40)*

*[PAUSE — 1 second]*

**"After six months on Quad360, a business that previously had no financial history has a complete, auditable financial profile — exactly what your credit team needs to say yes."** *(0:52)*

*[PAUSE — 2 seconds. Let that land.]*

**"For Zenith Bank, this means three things:"** *(0:55)*

**"A pre-qualified SME loan pipeline — with documentation already prepared."** *(1:00)*

**"A real-time monitoring layer for your existing SME borrowers — so you see stress coming before a payment is missed."** *(1:07)*

**"And access to hundreds of thousands of creditworthy SMEs that your current process cannot reach."** *(1:13)*

*[PAUSE — 1 second]*

**"We are not asking Zenith Bank to build anything. The technology is built, tested, and running."** *(1:20)*

*[PAUSE — 1 second]*

**"We are asking for thirty minutes, a pilot, and the chance to show you what this looks like with your own SME numbers."** *(1:28)*

*[PAUSE — 2 seconds. Smile slightly. Deliver the close with quiet confidence.]*

**"The businesses driving Nigeria's economy deserve to be seen clearly. And the bank that sees them first — wins them."** *(1:38)*

*[SIT. SILENCE. DO NOT FILL THE SILENCE.]*

## ANTICIPATED FIRST QUESTIONS — AND YOUR ANSWERS

**Q: How do you ensure data security?**
> "Quad360 uses end-to-end encryption for all financial data, Row-Level Security on our cloud database, two-factor authentication with fail-secure enforcement, and certificate pinning on all API calls. We are built to the same security standard as a financial institution — because we handle the same category of data."

**Q: How many users do you have?**
> "We are in active deployment and growing month on month. What matters more for this conversation is the quality of the data we generate — and I would like to show you a live financial profile from a real Quad360 business so you can see exactly what your credit team would be working with."

**Q: What do you need from Zenith Bank?**
> "Three things: distribution access to your SME customer base, a credit criteria definition so we know which businesses to pre-qualify for Zenith products, and a 90-day pilot agreement with a defined SME cohort. Everything else we handle."

**Q: Who else are you speaking to?**
> "We are in conversations with other tier-one institutions. We came to Zenith first because of your SME banking leadership position in Nigeria and your reputation for innovation. We would prefer to build this with Zenith Bank — but we are moving quickly."

**Q: What is the commercial arrangement?**
> "We have four models in the proposal — ranging from a simple revenue-share referral arrangement to a full white-label enterprise agreement. The right structure depends on your strategic priority. The full financial model is in the proposal document I have prepared for your team."
