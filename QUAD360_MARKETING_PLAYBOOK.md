### Full Feature Map

#### 🏠 DASHBOARD (Command Center)
The first screen every user sees. It surfaces:
- **Net Position** — total income vs. expenses at a glance
- **Money Owed to You** — outstanding invoices (accounts receivable)
- **Quick Add** — log income or expense in under 10 seconds via floating action button
- **Cash Pockets** — virtual envelopes for separating funds (rent pocket, tax pocket, savings pocket)
- **Daily Target Card** — daily revenue goal tracker
- **Goals Progress** — at-a-glance view of active financial goals
- **Monthly Review** — automated end-of-month summary
- **End-of-Day Report** — single tap to close out the business day
- **Global Search** — find any transaction, invoice, or record instantly
- **Onboarding Wizard** — guided setup for new users

---

#### 💳 TRANSACTIONS
- Log income and expenses with category, description, amount, and date
- 15+ income categories (Sales, Service, Consulting, Rental, Interest…)
- 10+ expense categories (Rent, Salaries, Utilities, Marketing, Transport…)
- Filter by date range, category, type
- Single-pass analytics: totals + category breakdown computed in real time
- Import bank CSV files (supports Nigerian bank date formats DD/MM/YYYY)

#### 🧾 INVOICES
- Create professional invoices with line items, tax, and due date
- Track status: Draft → Sent → Partial → Paid → Overdue
- Automatic overdue detection
- Outstanding balance summary
- Share invoices directly from the app

#### 💰 CASH FLOW
- 12-month cash flow forecast (projected inflows vs. outflows)
- Accounts receivable risk scoring by client
- Overdue AR flagged with aging analysis
- Burn rate calculation

#### 📊 REPORTS
- P&L (Profit & Loss) statement
- Revenue by category breakdown
- Expense breakdown
- Exportable summaries

#### 🎯 GOALS
- Set financial goals: save a target, reduce overdue AR, hit a revenue milestone
- Progress bar tracking with live baseline values
- Offline-safe: goal deletes queue when there's no connection

#### 🏦 BANK AGGREGATOR
- Connect your bank account (powered by Pngme)
- Automatic transaction sync
- Bank statement reconciliation — match imported bank CSV rows to logged transactions
- Supports Nigerian bank CSV formats

#### 💼 PAYROLL
- Add staff members with salary and deduction rates
- Run payroll and generate pay slips
- Payroll history by period

#### 🏗️ ASSETS
- Track business assets (equipment, vehicles, property)
- Depreciation calculation
- Asset value over time

#### 📦 INVENTORY
- Track stock levels by product
- Low-stock alerts
- Cost of goods tracking

#### 💳 LOANS
- Track business loans with interest rate and term
- Monthly payment calculator (PMT formula)
- Payment history and remaining balance
- Next due date tracking

#### 📈 ANALYSIS
- Break-even analysis (fixed costs, variable costs, price per unit)
- Margin of safety calculation
- Unit economics dashboard

#### 🧠 AI BUSINESS ADVISOR (CFO Screen)
- AI-powered business coach ("Your AI CFO")
- Natural language Q&A about your financials
- Actionable recommendations based on your actual data

#### 📉 GROWTH INTELLIGENCE
- Business growth metrics and KPIs
- Trend analysis across periods

#### ❤️ FINANCIAL HEALTH SCORE
- Powered by Pngme
- Credit-readiness score
- Liquidity, profitability, and solvency indicators

#### 🔗 PAYMENT LINKS
- Generate payment links to collect money from customers
- Share via WhatsApp, email, or social

#### ⚙️ SETTINGS
- Multi-currency support (₦ default, others available)

### How a User Operates the App — Step by Step

#### Day 1: Onboarding (5 minutes)
1. Download app → Sign up with email + password
2. Set up 2FA (SMS or authenticator)
3. First Run Wizard: enter business name, currency, industry
4. Optional: connect bank account via Pngme
5. Optional: import historical transactions via CSV

#### Daily Use (2–5 minutes/day)
1. Open app → Dashboard shows today's position
2. Tap **+** → Log income or expense as transactions happen
3. End of day → tap **End of Day** to close out and review
4. Check "Money Owed to You" → follow up on overdue invoices

#### Weekly Use (10–15 minutes)
1. Create invoices for services rendered
2. Mark paid invoices as paid
3. Review Cash Flow forecast — is there a shortfall coming?
4. Check Goals progress

#### Monthly Use (30 minutes)
1. Run Reconciliation — match bank statement to logged transactions
2. Review Reports (P&L, category breakdown)
3. Run Payroll for staff
4. Review Financial Health Score
5. Ask AI Advisor: "Where am I losing money this month?"

## PART 2: MARKETING STRATEGY

### Target Customer Profiles

#### Primary: The Hustling SME Owner
- **Who**: Sole trader or small business owner with 1–20 employees
- **Where**: Lagos, Abuja, Port Harcourt, Accra, Nairobi
- **Pain**: Manages finances in WhatsApp notes or exercise books. No idea of profit. Chases invoices manually. Payroll is a monthly headache.
- **Trigger**: "I don't know if my business is making money"

#### Secondary: The Growing Retailer / Service Business
- **Who**: Pharmacy, salon, fashion brand, logistics company, contractor
- **Pain**: Has a POS but no P&L. Inventory goes missing. Staff payments are inconsistent. Bank reconciliation takes a week.
- **Trigger**: First audit request, tax season, or a banker asking for financial statements

#### Tertiary: The Investor / Lender (B2B)
- **Who**: Angel investor, microfinance institution, accelerator, bank
- **Pain**: SME portfolio has no financial data. Can't assess creditworthiness.
- **Value prop**: Quad360 is the data layer — every SME using it generates a financial track record investable by institutions.

### Investor Pitch Angles

#### The Problem (Market Size)
- 41 million SMEs in Nigeria alone (NBS 2023)
- 90%+ have no formal financial records
- Less than 3% have access to formal credit — primarily because they can't prove financial health
- African SME financing gap: **$331 billion** (IFC)

#### The Solution
Quad360 is the financial infrastructure layer for African SMEs — the operating system that:
1. Generates the financial data these businesses have never had
2. Creates the track record that unlocks credit
3. Connects SMEs to financial services (lending, insurance, payments) through that data

#### The Business Model
- SaaS subscription (recurring revenue)
- Payment processing revenue share (Paystack/Flutterwave integration)
- Financial data licensing to lenders and MFIs (highest margin, B2B2C)
- Embedded lending: Quad360 users with proven revenue records get pre-approved loan offers in-app

#### Traction Metrics to Highlight
- Daily Active Users (DAU) / Monthly Active Users (MAU)
- Transactions logged per user per month (engagement depth)
- Invoice volume processed (GMV proxy)
- Payroll runs processed
- MoM growth rate

#### Comparable Companies (for investor context)
- **Wave** (West Africa) — payments, but no financial management
- **Kippa** (Nigeria) — bookkeeping only, no payroll/loans/invoicing
- **Zoho Books** / **QuickBooks** — too complex, not Africa-native
- **Quad360 = the gap**: African-native, mobile-first, all-in-one financial OS
